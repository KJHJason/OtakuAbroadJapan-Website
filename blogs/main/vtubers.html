About Vtubers

A vtuber basically refers to any fictional character that often livestreams regularly talking or playing games or singing and dancing. It is basically an online entertainer represented by a digital avatar generated by computer graphics or other means. The most famous examples are Hololive, NIJISANJI, and various independent vtubers such as Kizuna Ai.

So... when did vtubers come about in Japan? Vtubers became a trend in the mid-2010s, originating from Japan. Regarding the controversial question, "Who is the first vtuber?", it is widely accepted that Kizuna Ai is the first vtuber among the vtubers community as she was the first vtuber to get famous. However, the are a few that disagree and would claim that Annoying Orange or the UK-based Japanese vlogger Ami Yamato is the first vtuber but I do not consider Annoying Orange as a vtuber. Personally, I guess Ami Yamato is technically one of the first few vtubers based on how you define a vtuber but I do think that Kizuna Ai is the first vtuber as she strongly reflects what a modern vtuber would do.

That being said, there are no subtitles for vtubers' livestreams, especially those who only speaks in Japanese. Hence, if you are not fluent in Japanese language, you might wonder whether vtubers is for you. However, do not worry, there are English speaking vtubers too! Other than that, if you really want to watch a Japanese speaking vtuber but do not understand what they are saying, you can watch translated clips of them.

Hololive

Hololive Production or Hololive for short, is a vtuber talent agency owned by Japanese tech entertainment company, Cover Corporation. Hololive was founded in December 2, 2019, and currently has over 50 members. It is one of the most popular vtuber group to watch.

Independent Vtubers

Independent vtubers basically refers to any vtubers that is not in a group. They usually manage themselves which is tough as they have to come up with own schedule and check if it is appropriate on their streaming platform. Additionally, a vtuber model could cost up to several thousands of dollars.
