About Japanese Music

Japanese music or music produced in Japan includes a plethora of distinct genres, both traditional and modern. However, I will mostly cover the modern side of Japanese music and the three main genres, Anisong, Jpop and some Japanese musicians that I personally like.

Behind the history of Japanese music, Japan is one of the largest markets for music on physical media in the world. It is worth US$2 billion in 2014, and the second-largest overall music market, with a retail value of US$2.6 billion in 2014.

Although I would not go into much detail about traditional Japanese music, some examples of traditional and folk music that you might be interested in are wadaiko, gagaku, hougaku, Min'y≈ç folk music and more that can date back to the Nara (710 to 794) and Heian (794 to 1185) period.

Around the late 19th century, came the influence of western music which helped shape the music industry today in Japan! Popular music nowadays includes a vast variety of genres such as Jpop, Anisong which I will be covering in-depth and recommend my personal favourites. Additionally, popular music also includes idol music, rock, heavy metal, kawaii future bass, and more!
