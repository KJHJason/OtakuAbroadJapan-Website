All About Hololive Production

Last updated: July 2, 2021

Introduction

Hololive Production (ホロライブプロダクション Hororaibu Purodakushon), or simply known as hololive (ホロライブ), is a Virtual Idol agency consisting of mainly female Virtual YouTubers supported and created by Cover Corp. Additionally, Hololive was founded on December 2, 2019, and currently has over 50 members. It is one of the most popular vtuber groups to watch. There are also sub-branches of Hololive which are Hololive JP, Hololive ID, Hololive CN, Hololive EN, and Holostars. Each of the sub-branches caters towards people from different countries, JP for Japanese, ID for Indonesians, Holostars (Male Vtubers) for the Japanese female fanbase, and EN for English speakers. Unfortunately, Hololive CN has been dissolved due to a controversial incident that caused the China fanbase to turn against Hololive.

1. Watame Tsunomaki [Hololive JP]


Portrait image from Hololive Production's official website.

Profile: Tsunomaki Watame, a sheep bard that goes around everywhere. She loves singing and is fluffy! YES! FLUFFY! Being a herbivore, she loves potato chips.

  • Youtube Channel: Watame Ch. 角巻わため
  • Debut date: December 29, 2019
  • Birthday: June 6th
  • Height: 151cm
  • Fanbase name: Wata-mate
  • Illustrator: Fu-mi

Personal comments: I would watch Watame as she is a "wholesome" sheep and is one of my personal favourites. I like her personality and she would put a smile on my face. She would speak in a pleasant tone and sounds especially soft, mature and womanly. However, she would also make some crazy facial expressions such as the very famous facial expression, "gangimari". Additionally, she can be a pervert at times which earned her the nickname, Wataoji. She is also famous for her headbangs which has become one of her signature gestures. I would usually watch her singing streams and listen to her cover songs. If you enjoy watching a wholesome and/or enjoy listening to cover songs, Watame is the one for you!

2. Momosuzu Nene [Hololive JP]


Portrait image from Hololive Production's official website.

Profile: A girl who came from another world to chase after her idol dream. She likes singing, dancing, and drawing. She trains hard every day in order to win the hearts of even more people.

  • Youtube Channel: Nene Ch.桃鈴ねね
  • Debut date: August 13, 2020
  • Birthday: March 2nd
  • Height: 159cm
  • Illustrator: Nishizawa 5mm

Personal comments: Nene is a cheerful girl who loves to talk and sing. Her dream is to become an idol and have her own anime opening song. She can be overconfident about her gaming skills and would call herself "Supernenechi". She also likes to flirt with other members of hololive by using her "handsome" voice or sending virtual kiss sounds. Other than that, she never gives up, she apparently applied for hololive four times, with the initial three applications not having any reply. I personally find that inspiring and like her personality for being a confident girl. I would usually watch her Minecraft streams and her singing streams too. If you like a cheeky and confident vtuber, Nene is the one for you. Oh, and she has her own husband collection lol.

3. Ninomae Ina'nis [Hololive EN]


Portrait image from Hololive Production's official website.

Profile: One day, Ina'nis picked up a strange book and then started to gain the power of controlling tentacles. To her, tentacles are just a part in her ordinary life; it has never been a big deal for her. However, her girly mind does want to get them dressed up and stay pretty. After gaining power, she started hearing Ancient Whispers and Revelations. Hence, she began her VTuber activities to deliver random sanity checks on humanity, as an ordinary girl.

  • Youtube Channel: Ninomae Ina'nis Ch. hololive-EN
  • Debut date: September 13, 2020
  • Birthday: May 20th
  • Height: 157cm
  • Illustrator: Kuroboshi Kouhaku

Personal comments: Ina has a peaceful and gentle personality. When Takanashi Kiara was feeling down, Ina is always the first to message her. Oh and Ina is infamous for her puns and her "dad jokes". She is also very skilled at drawing illustrations and would give tips to aspiring artists like me. I would usually watch her drawing streams and various game streams because of her calm personality. If you also like to draw anime-style illustrations or wants to watch a chill vtuber, Ina would be your cup of tea.

4. Inugami Korone [Hololive JP]


Portrait image from Hololive Production's official website.

Profile: "G'morning! Fingers! Give me your fingers!!" The dog from a random bakery. She likes to play games when she has free time while watchdogging.

  • Youtube Channel: Korone Ch. 戌神ころね
  • Debut date: April 13, 2019
  • Birthday: October 1st
  • Height: 156cm
  • Fanbase name: Koronesuki
  • Illustrator: Fukahire

Personal comments: Korone debuted as part of the group, "Hololive Gamers" along with Shirakami Fubuki, Ookami Mio and Nekomata Okayu. Korone is a wholesome vtuber who frequently will have teetee moments with Nekomata Okayu. The two are always together and the pair is known as "OkaKoro". Additionally, she loves to play retro games so if you are into retro games, you would definitely want to watch her streams. You also might notice her profile, "Give me your fingers!!". Well, it originated from her desire to have her own unique greeting. She joked about greeting her viewers by asking for their fingers in which they would reply "Yubi! Yubi!" (which means "Finger! Finger!") in return. Hence, make sure to keep your fingers protected from Korone lol. Other than that, she has a sweet and goofy personality and is usually wholesome (be blessed if she sneezes) but can be quite terrifying at times. Lastly, she also tries her best to speak English with English speaking fans.
