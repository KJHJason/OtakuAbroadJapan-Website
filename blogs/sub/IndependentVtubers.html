All About Indepedent Vtubers

Last updated: June 23, 2021

Introduction

Independent vtubers basically refers to any vtubers that is not in a group. They usually manage themselves which is tough as they have to come up with their own schedule and check if it is appropriate on their streaming platform, mainly YouTube or Twitch. Additionally, a high-quality vtuber model could cost up to several thousands of dollars. It is a big investment which could either go both ways, being successful or being outshined by other competitors.

Additional notes: The profile description of the respective vtubers in this website are official profile description from their YouTube channel description or Twitch.

Disclaimer for the images used: All images used here are not owned by OtakuAbroadJapan, they are the official vtuber portrait image from the respective vtuber.
